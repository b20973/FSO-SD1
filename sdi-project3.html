Project 2

A hobby of mine that I enjoy just as much as sand gardening is attending local film festivals. When the Cleveland International Film Fest comes to town, my friends and I always try to buy our tickets in advance. Purchasing a package ahead of time ensures two things, getting them at a better rate and avoiding sold out shows.

Purchasing my tickets in advance also gives me the opportunity to "make a night" out of it. Touring downtown Cleveland and checking out new restaurants and bars makes the occasion a bit more special.

March 21st 2013, Brandon Lee Scott
